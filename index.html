Careers at KraneShares

Headquartered in New York City, KraneShares' vision is to create a company where people feel valued, productive, and challenged. To do that, we want to be a workplace that represents everyone. All our employees have the opportunity to collaborate, connect, and be heard.

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

KraneShares is an inclusive culture dedicated to continuously fostering a sense of belonging where the different opinions and perspectives of our team members are welcome; no matter their level, role, department or office location. We recognize and respect the importance of diversity and appreciate the variety experience, background, thought, and behavior our employees bring to the company. We also know that diverse teams make for better ideas, a better company, and a better world.

If you have a strong work ethic, a distinct point of view and thrive in a fast paced and innovative financial services environment, please review our job openings to see if any open roles match your experience and interests. We encourage candidates from traditionally underrepresented communities to apply.

Our Culture

At KraneShares you will not only learn from our executives but also from your peers. Our mission is to provide a stimulating and supportive environment, that inspires challenging work and a collaborative culture.

Investing in Our People

By investing in our talent and providing tools that serve them both in and out of the office, KraneShares creates a happier and more productive workplace culture. We regularly engage our employees to continuously understand their unique learning needs and growth goals. This engagement allows to curate professional development resources that are tailored to the real-time needs and interests of our teams. Our employee retention is high because we encourage taking the initiative to learn and grow and value the contributions of all our employees.

Staying Connected

We also know how to have a little fun. As a friendly, tight-knit group we host regular company gatherings in and out of the office. In an effort to adapt to an increasingly remote world, we have recently kicked off virtual happy hours ensuring that even our out-of-office employees feel connected to their colleagues on a regular basis.

Employee Perks

KraneShares’ management also strives to provide our employees with a work-life balance that includes a flexible work-from-home policy, generous paid time off and progressive parental leave options.

Additionally, the company fosters a friendly, tight-knit environment by hosting regular company gatherings, most recently virtual happy hours.
